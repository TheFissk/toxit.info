About us

The danger of hate speech when broadcast over social media has been well documented. Social media brings together like minded individuals and creates echo chambers of opinion which can lead to more extreme opinions. We see this in Reddit where toxicity in a subreddit encourages other users of that subreddit to post more toxic content. To date efforts have focused on either censorship by removing subreddits or research to understand toxicity. We aim to empower users to create change by letting them view comment toxicity in individual subreddits, and visualize known correlations to toxicity between subreddits such as moderators and commenters.
